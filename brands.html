Popular use cases

See how brands are using Nceno to boost customer engagement and retention.

  • Treehouse Brewing
    Brews for BPM's: Frequent customers get an invite code to a challenge. 10 workouts logged = 1 free beer.
  • Startup Accelerator
    Get fit, get featured: Startup teams pool their activity minutes to earn Accelerator tokens. 1000 minutes = featurette in the monthly investor newsletter
  • Mud Racers
    No train, no gain: Competitors buy an invite code to a lead-up challenge. Every workout logged is redeemable for a portion of the admission ticket with potential to earn a profit from slackers.
