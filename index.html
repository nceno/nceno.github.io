Set a goal, stake some cash, workout for a payout.

Nceno lets you put a sort of security deposit on your goal so that achieving it becomes a priority, not a daydream. As you progress, you earn a little back each time. Skip a workout, and it's gonna cost you.

Trustless group challenges from anywhere.

Anyone in the world can see your goal and join you with money of their own. Now, when anyone in your group skips a workout, the rest get to split their loss as reward! Nceno makes your workouts work for you.

Powered by Ethereum.

Using the Ethereum blockchain allows us to reach people where banks and credit cards sometimes can't... and probably never will. It also keeps personal health data and financial data separate and private (as they should be).
