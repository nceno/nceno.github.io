How Nceno works

Nceno uses a combination of IoT (your fitness tracker) and Blockchain to achieve trustless verification and immediate payouts for your goal progress. All you need is a fitness tracker with a heart rate sensor, and some Ethereum (cryptocurrency).

Step one

Buy some crypto (ethereum) and link your fitness tracker.

Step two

Set (or join) a challenge, and stake some cash (using crypto). The challenge starts whenever you want it to.

Step three

Log your workout with the press of a button to trigger an instant payout for your progress, based on readings from your fitness tracker.

Step four

If you skip a workout, you don't get a payout, and that bit goes to the people who worked out.

Step five

Claim lost stake from those who skipped. Their loss is your profit!
